Solar Tracking System

1 - Project Description :

Solar Tracking System is a solar tracker system based only on analog and digitial electronics (no microcontroller) , the main goal is the charge the maximum voltage of solar panels based on the motion of the sun , and at night it activate the second system which is an irrigation system based on 4 dc motors that works synchronsulty .
