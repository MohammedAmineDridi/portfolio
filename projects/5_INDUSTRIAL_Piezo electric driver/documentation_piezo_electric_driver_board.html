Piezoelectric Driver Board

- The goal is to create a high voltage circuit driver for piezoelectric elements , it can be used in many applications such as : inkjet piezoelectric printhead drivers/...

- This circuit has a built-in function generator with controlled (amplitude,frequency) which can produce (square,triangular and sine) waveforms using 1 op-amp in mono-stable mode to generate a square wave and 2 integrators op-amps to produce the triangle and sine wave with 4 potentiometers to set the desired (frequency and amplitude) to drive any AC load with specific parameters .Also , it has an 8 bit shift register to driver 8 I/O pins up to +40V for piezoelectric loads .
